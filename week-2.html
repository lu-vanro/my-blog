Retrospective:

What went well?

  • • Completed four out of five interviews and gathered valuable insights.
  • • Identified a final insight to focus on after analysing the interviews.
  • • Started working on a solution for the brief.
  • • Prepared and submitted slides for the Work in Progress presentation.
  • • Coded my blog and documented all my standup notes, keeping everything organised.

What didn’t go well?

  • • The fifth interview didn't record due to privacy restrictions on the recording app. I conducted the interview over a phone call, but the app blocks audio from regular phone calls, which I only discovered afterwards.
  • • Lost the audio and couldn't retrieve it; I was also unable to re-interview the participant. I've moved forward with only four interviews instead of five.
  • • Work and caring for my partner, who has been ill with the flu, added extra strain and limited my time for university tasks.

What can be improved?

  • • Double-check the recording setup, especially for phone interviews, to avoid similar issues in the future.
  • • Once my partner recovers and my workload eases, I'll be in a better position to balance university, work and personal responsibilities.

What will I try next week?

  • • I will try to limit the amount of work on my plate next week and adjust my schedule to make the most of any free time available.
