A weekly blog documenting how I progress with the 'New Enterprises' assignment.

The Brief

It’s the year 2027. As a recent graduate of Media Design School, you decide to put your design skills toward setting up a new social and/or commercial enterprise in Auckland, New Zealand.

Demonstrate the important contribution design can make to social, cultural and community well-being, quality of life and identity. Use a lean human-centred design approach to determine the concept and scope of the enterprise, and the interactive product or service that this enterprise offers.

Your designed solution must relate to an identified present-day, real world problem.

You will need to consider an appropriate target market carefully so that you can tailor your solution, test appropriately and promote your enterprise across relevant media channels.

Pitch your idea with a presentation and a design strategy document, reflect on your weekly design sprints, and present your final response.
