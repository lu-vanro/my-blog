Retrospective:

What went well?

  • • Finalised UX research including personas and user journeys.
  • • Created a formal competitor analysis and drafted a business model.
  • • Made good progress on the proof of concept and UI inspiration.
  • • Started designing slides and the design strategy document in preparation for next week’s pitch.
  • • Blog is up to date with all stand-up notes.

    • What didn’t go well?

    • • Struggled with understanding the deliverables for the brief and proof of concept mid-week. It slowed progress slightly until I was able to clarify things.
    • • Haven’t made as much progress on the Type Summative as I should have.

    What can be improved?

    • • Try to stay on top of all project tasks (especially the Type Summative) to avoid a backlog.
    • • When deliverables feel overwhelming or unclear, break them into smaller steps sooner to prevent momentum loss.

    What will I try next week?

    • • Keep refining the proof of concept and presentation slides.
    • • Begin working on wireframes or prototypes to communicate the solution more clearly.
    • • Dedicate focused time to catching up on the Type Summative and ensure it doesn’t get left behind.
